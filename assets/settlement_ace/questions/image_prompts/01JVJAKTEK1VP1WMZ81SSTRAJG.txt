The garden is lush with greenery, featuring meticulously manicured hedges and vibrant flowerbeds filled with pastel-colored roses, lilies, and lavender. The sky above is a soft, watercolour wash of pale blues and pinks, giving the scene a sense of historical grandeur and the passage of time.
