A faint, almost ethereal, map of the United Kingdom is subtly integrated into the wallpaper behind the Chancellor, hinting at the geographic scope of his economic decisions. The colour palette leans towards pastel blues, reds, and golds, subtly referencing the UK flag while maintaining a calming and professional aesthetic.
