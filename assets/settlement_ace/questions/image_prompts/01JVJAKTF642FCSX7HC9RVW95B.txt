The globe is rendered with soft, muted blues and greens to depict land and water. The interconnected lines are colored in a pale red, creating a visual contrast and emphasizing the global connectivity. The composition is balanced, with both the television and the globe being of equal visual weight. Subtle shading adds depth and dimension to the elements. The background is a soft, gradient blend of pastel blues and whites, evoking a sense of innovation and progress. The overall style should be reminiscent of classic scientific illustrations, emphasizing clarity and detail.
