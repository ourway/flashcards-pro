On the right, an adult figure, clearly over 18, confidently strides towards the entrance, the barrier dissipating before them. The adult carries a neatly folded newspaper featuring horse racing headlines. The overall mood is one of quiet anticipation and the understanding of legal boundaries, with the clock tower acting as a symbolic reminder of the age requirement. The style should be reminiscent of classic British illustrations, with attention to detail in the architectural elements and clothing of the figures.
