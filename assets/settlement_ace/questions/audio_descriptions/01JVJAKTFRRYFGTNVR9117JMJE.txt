Age UK is the leading charity in the United Kingdom dedicated to supporting people in later life. Understanding its role requires knowing a bit about the challenges older individuals face. As people age, they may encounter issues like loneliness, financial difficulties, health problems, and difficulty accessing essential services. Age UK works to combat these challenges through various initiatives, including providing information and advice, offering practical support, campaigning for policy changes, and running local services. The organization was formed in 2009 through the merger of Age Concern England and Help the Aged, consolidating their efforts to better serve the aging population.
