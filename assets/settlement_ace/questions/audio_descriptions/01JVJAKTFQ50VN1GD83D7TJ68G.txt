London's West End is globally renowned as a hub for live theatre, drawing millions of visitors each year. This area, concentrated around the City of Westminster, boasts a high density of theatres staging everything from classic plays to cutting-edge musicals. The term "Theatreland" naturally emerged to reflect this central characteristic.
