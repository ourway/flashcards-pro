Okay, let's talk about some of the UK's most renowned galleries. When you think of art in the UK, you're really thinking about institutions that have a national presence and hold collections of significant historical and cultural importance. The National Gallery in London, for example, houses a vast collection of Western European paintings from the 13th to the 19th centuries. Tate Britain focuses on British art from 1500 to the present day, while its sister gallery, Tate Modern, is dedicated to international modern and contemporary art. These are national institutions, funded to preserve and display art for the public.
