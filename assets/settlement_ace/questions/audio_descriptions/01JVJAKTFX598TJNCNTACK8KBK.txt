Football, often called soccer in some parts of the world, holds a special place in the heart of the United Kingdom. Its roots run deep, stretching back to the mid-19th century when the Football Association was formed in 1863, standardizing the rules of the game. While rugby, golf, and tennis are certainly popular sports in the UK, none command the same widespread passion and participation as football.
