Over time, it transformed into a day to honour mothers. The key to understanding the gifts lies in the sentiment behind the day. It's about showing appreciation and love in a simple, heartfelt way. Flowers symbolize beauty and growth, reflecting a mother's nurturing role. Greeting cards offer a personal message of thanks, and chocolates are a sweet treat to express affection. New clothes or shoes, while potentially appreciated, are less traditional and lack the personal, sentimental touch associated with Mothering Sunday. The focus is on small, thoughtful gestures rather than extravagant presents.
