Okay, let's talk about responsible dog ownership in the UK. Think about it this way: if your dog gets lost, how will anyone know who to contact? That's where the collar comes in. The law requires that dogs in public places wear a collar with the owner's name and address. This isn't just a suggestion; it's the law. This allows anyone who finds your dog to quickly identify and contact you, ensuring your furry friend gets home safely. While a special dog coat might be cute, and keeping your dog close is generally good practice, and even socializing with other dogs is great, these aren't legal requirements for identification purposes.
